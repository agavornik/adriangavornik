About

I am a researcher specializing in AI ethics and technology assessment, with a background in Science, Technology and Society Studies (STS). My work focuses on methodological aspects of technology assessments, particularly ethics-based assessments of AI systems, and how AI practitioners navigate ethical challenges in their research and work.
